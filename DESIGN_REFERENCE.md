# CitadelView — Design Reference

> Reference image: `citadel-reference.png` (see repo root)

## Visual Style
- Isometric / top-down pixel art office
- Light stone/cream colour palette — walls are pale grey-white brick
- Warm wooden furniture (desks, shelves, filing cabinets)
- Lush green plants scattered throughout for atmosphere
- Soft pastel lighting — no harsh shadows

## Room Layout (matches the image)

### Top-Left — WAR ROOM (Strategy Room)
- Large octagonal conference table with chairs
- Wall map with network/pin connections
- Computer workstations along side wall
- Shelving units and clipboards on walls
- Plants in corners

### Top-Right — THE LIBRARY
- Floor-to-ceiling bookshelves (brown, packed with books)
- Arched windows with data visualisations visible through glass
- Central reading desk with open book and computer
- Warm lamp lighting

### Centre-Top — Corridor / Commons Area
- Stone steps connecting upper and lower levels
- Blue banners with "B" insignia hanging from pillars
- Potted plants and benches — social/waiting area

### Centre — BUREAU COMMONS (Open Atrium)
- Central stone fountain with water feature
- Outdoor-style lamp posts
- Small collaborative tables and chairs
- Open tile floor — gathering space between rooms

### Bottom-Left — WRITING DESK / Data Room
- Multiple large monitor screens showing dashboards and charts
- Dark ambient lighting — focused work area
- Single analyst chair
- Data visualisation wall

### Bottom-Right — THE VAULT
- Filing cabinet arrays (grey metal, floor-to-ceiling)
- Secure-looking terminal/kiosk
- Warm lamp, plant, desk with computer
- Feels like a records/compliance room

## Agents (visual targets for future sprite work)
Each agent should eventually be a small pixel character that fits the isometric aesthetic. For the prototype, coloured circles are fine, but the long-term vision is characters walking the corridors with idle animations.

- Accent blue: `#2B5FA0` (banners, screens)
- Plant green: `#4A7C3F`
